Boulevardier

Description

Make a classic boulevardier cocktail if you're a fan of negronis. It's similar, but made with bourbon instead of gin for a more harmonious blend

Ingredients

  • 25ml bourbon
  • 25ml sweet vermouth
  • 25ml Campari
  • ice

Steps

  1. Pour the bourbon, vermouth and Campari into a mixing glass, jug or cocktail shaker. Add a large handful of ice, and stir until the outside of the glass feels very cold
  2. Strain the cocktail into an ice-filled tumbler, and garnish with a twist of orange peel
